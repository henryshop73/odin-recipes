Dad's Spaghetti

Delicious Dad's Spaghetti

Enjoyable dish that here we will teach you to prepare in the same style and good taste of Italian food

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 2 cans condensed tomato soup
  • 2 1/2 cups water

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to minutes or until al dente; drain
  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook ground beef with onion until meat is brown. Drain
  4. Return meat mixture to pan with tomato paste, tomato soup and water. Cook stirring, until heated through and thickened, 5 to 15 minutes
  5. Serve over cooked pasta

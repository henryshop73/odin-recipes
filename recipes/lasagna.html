Healthy Lighter Lasagna

This is a lighter version of a classic lasagna that we will show you the steps and the ingredients that you will use so that you can later enjoy it with the family

Ingredients

  • 1 package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 cup low-fat cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Cooking spray
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 large garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups chopped mushrooms
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 pound ground turkey breast
  • 1 jar low-fat pasta sauce (such as Healthy request)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 6 no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup shredded low-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup tomato-vegetable juice coctail

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Mix the spinach, cottage cheese and egg in a large bowl.
  2. Place a large skillet over medium-hight heat, and coat with nonstick cooking spray. Cook the onion, garlic, mushrooms and carrot until the onion is soft, about 5 minutes
  3. Add the gound turkey an stir until cooked through about 5 minutes
  4. Pour the pasta sauce and water into the skillet. Season with rosemary, oregano and basil. Simmer sauce for 10 minutes.
  5. Spray a 9x13 baking pan with nonstick cooking spray. spread 1/3 of the souce on the bottom of the pan and top with 3 noodles
  6. Spoon an additional 1/3 of the sauce on the noodles
  7. Top with remaining 3 noodles and the rest of the sauce
  8. Pour the vegetable juice evenly over the lasagna and top with the remaining mozzarella. Cover the dishi with foil
  9. Bked in the preheated for 45 minutes. remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes
  10. Remove from oven and let lasagna rest for 10 to 15 minutes before serving.
